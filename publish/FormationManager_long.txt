[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Formation Manager is a utility mod that allows players to assign specific troop types to specific formations (1-8) directly in the party screen. The assignments are persistent and automatically applied when entering battles, preventing the game from auto-shuffling your troops.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Installation instructions[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]1. Extract the downloaded archive into your Mount & Blade II Bannerlord game directory (specifically inside the Modules folder).
2. Ensure the "FormationManager" folder is present under Modules.
3. Enable "Stop Shuffling, You Fools - Formation Manager" in the game launcher.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Main features[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Party Screen Badges: Assign troops to formation numbers (I-VIII) directly from the party screen.
- Persistent Assignments: Assignments are saved automatically per campaign main hero.
- Fixed Spawning: Troops spawn in and stay in their designated formations.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Harmony
- UIExtenderEx
- MCM (Mod Configuration Menu)[/color][/size]
